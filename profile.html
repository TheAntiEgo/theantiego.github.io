Josh D

I am a multifaceted designer and entrepreneur with five years of digital and traditional graphic design and illustration work to my name. I currently am actively involved in art directing and managing the brand identities of three local companies, Band, ADX, and Portland Made. Band, a company which I co-founded, is a design studio that focuses on creating meaningful brand identities and public presences for new companies using graphic design, web development and clever marketing as our main tools. ADX is a bootstrapped, community-based makerspace in the heart of industrial SE Portland. Portland Made is an advocacy group for Urban Manufacturing in the city of Portland.
